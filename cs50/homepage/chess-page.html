Chess

Only recently I developed an affinity for chess. During quarantine, I was bored an decided to take out the old chess board and play against my older brother. After I beat him, quite easily, I realized that I might have a natural talent for the game. I downloaded the chess.com app and built up my rating, getting better and better every day. Fast forward to now, where I will be participating in my first in-person chess tournament in about a few weeks. Yes, my passion for chess grew very fast!
